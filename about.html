My Story

My name is Faezeh Ghorbani, and I am a 30-year-old graphic designer and photographer. I have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Industrial Design, which equipped me with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in my field.

I have been working as a graphic designer for the past 3 years, serving different clients with my creative expertise and design solutions. My proficiency in software tools such as Photoshop, Premier, Illustrator, and InDesign, has allowed me to create visually stunning designs that cater to client requirements.

My passion for photography has also aided me in enhancing my creative portfolio. I specialize in portrait and product photography, lending my expert eye to capture beautiful and engaging images for my clients. Whether it's a product shoot for an e-commerce site or a portrait session for a business professional, I always aim to deliver photos that surpass expectations.

I believe that my attention to detail, creativity, and problem-solving skills, make me an asset to the design industry. I'm excited to continue to develop my skills and gain new experiences in both graphic design and photography fields.
